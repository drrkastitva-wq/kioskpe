# Daily Needs of Advocates in India (Practical Feature List)

## Must-Have Daily Workflows
- Case diary and hearing notes
- Next hearing/date reminders
- Daily board: today's hearings, deadlines, tasks
- Client communication log (calls, meetings, updates)
- Filing checklist by matter type
- Drafting from pre-approved templates
- Court and police contact lookup

## High-Value Utilities
- Limitation period calculator
- Court fee/stamp fee helper (state-wise, later phase)
- Cause list watcher (court-specific)
- Evidence/document checklist tracker
- Opposite counsel and party detail tracker
- Billing and payment follow-up tracker

## Team/Office Operations
- Clerk task management and follow-up tracking
- Matter allocation by advocate practice area
- Centralized precedent bank (orders, arguments, notes)
- Document version history and audit trail

## Growth/Client Service
- Client portal (view hearing dates and basic case updates)
- Automated update messages after hearing outcomes
- Referral/source tracking for new client intake

## Compliance & Security
- Role-based access and strict permissions
- Bar Council ID verification for legal-content access
- Data encryption + backups
- Immutable audit logs for sensitive changes

## Future AI Features (After MVP)
- Draft summarization
- Hearing prep checklists from case history
- Legal research assist over internal document repository
- Smart reminders based on case stage
